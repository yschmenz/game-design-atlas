The systems & mechanics wing — opened by schmenz, July 2026, built on the conceptual
arc of Jesse Schell's *The Art of Game Design: A Book of Lenses* (topics phrased in our
own words; grab the book for the original lens questions).

20 core topics in five clusters: **Experience & Feel** (1–4), **Motivation** (5–8),
**Mechanics & Systems** (9–14), **Player & Interface** (15–17), **Meaning & Structure** (18–20).
