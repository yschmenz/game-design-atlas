# Recommended candidates (draft v2 — deep cuts)

Previous draft was too mainstream. This one stays in Cosmo D territory: small-dev, surreal, jazz/ambient-scored, liminal-space wandering games. None of these are in the current 46.

## Cosmo D himself

- **Off-Peak** — his first game (2015, free). Same universe as The Norwood Suite: a surreal train station bazaar, first pass at his wandering-collage style. https://store.steampowered.com/app/467360/OffPeak/
- **Tales from Off-Peak City Vol. 1** — same universe, first-person vignettes, deepens the world Norwood Suite is already logged for. https://store.steampowered.com/app/1129920/Tales_From_OffPeak_City_Vol_1/
- **Betrayal at Club Low** — his latest (2023): dice-rolled dialogue, jazz-noir nightclub mystery, still the same fictional city. https://store.steampowered.com/app/1885750/Betrayal_At_Club_Low/

## Jazz / cosmic surrealism

- **Genesis Noir** — Miles Davis-inspired cosmic jazz surrealism, the Big Bang told as a noir love story. https://store.steampowered.com/app/735290/Genesis_Noir/
- **Superbrothers: Sword & Sworcery EP** — moody, minimalist, Jim Guthrie soundtrack; cult classic sound-design touchstone. https://store.steampowered.com/app/204060/Superbrothers_Sword__Sworcery_EP/

## Absurdist / comedic surrealism

- **Jazzpunk** — deadpan spy satire, dreamlike non-sequitur vignettes. https://store.steampowered.com/app/250260/Jazzpunk_Directors_Cut/
- **Sludge Life** — first-person skating/graffiti surreal open world, punk aesthetic. https://store.steampowered.com/app/1144770/SLUDGE_LIFE/
- **Extreme Meatpunks Forever** — anarchic, mech-dating-sim-adjacent, punk narrative delivery. Not on Steam — itch.io only: https://hth.itch.io/extreme-meatpunks-forever

## Liminal / fragmented horror-adjacent

- **Paratopic** — VHS-glitch liminal horror, deliberately fragmented narrative. https://store.steampowered.com/app/897030/Paratopic/
- **Mundaun** — hand-pencil-drawn folk horror, Swiss Alps setting. https://store.steampowered.com/app/720350/Mundaun/
- **The Longing** — solitary real-time meditative wandering (a 400-day single mechanic). https://store.steampowered.com/app/893850/THE_LONGING/

## Analgesic Productions (dream-logic auteur)

- **Anodyne** — surreal dream-logic Zelda-like. https://store.steampowered.com/app/234900/Anodyne/
- **Anodyne 2** — shifts between dream and reality states. https://store.steampowered.com/app/877810/Anodyne_2_Return_to_Dust/
- **All Our Asias** — experimental structure, a memory-train narrative. https://store.steampowered.com/app/698050/All_Our_Asias/
- **Even the Ocean** — political/emotional platformer narrative. https://store.steampowered.com/app/265470/Even_the_Ocean/

## Quiet / minimalist narrative

- **Wide Ocean Big Jacket** — quiet family camping trip, minimalist dialogue. https://store.steampowered.com/app/1198040/Wide_Ocean_Big_Jacket/
- **Wilmot's Warehouse** — abstract, meditative sorting-as-puzzle. https://store.steampowered.com/app/839870/Wilmots_Warehouse/
- **milk outside a bag of milk outside a bag of milk** — surreal, personal, very short. https://store.steampowered.com/app/1604000/Milk_outside_a_bag_of_milk_outside_a_bag_of_milk/
- **Strange Horticulture** — small-scale atmospheric narrative puzzle. https://store.steampowered.com/app/1574580/Strange_Horticulture/

## Metanarrative / genre pastiche

- **The Hex** — Daniel Mullins pastiching his own back catalog, metanarrative. https://store.steampowered.com/app/510420/The_Hex/
- **El Paso, Elsewhere** — Max Payne pastiche gone surreal noir. https://store.steampowered.com/app/1546310/El_Paso_Elsewhere/
- **We Know the Devil** — unsettling, tightly-scoped visual novel. https://store.steampowered.com/app/435300/We_Know_the_Devil/

## Cryptic worldbuilding

- **Umurangi Generation** — photography mechanic, dense punk-cyberpunk lore delivered obliquely. https://store.steampowered.com/app/1223500/Umurangi_Generation/
- **Dujanah** — surreal stop-motion claymation, grief/war allegory. https://store.steampowered.com/app/681240/Dujanah/
