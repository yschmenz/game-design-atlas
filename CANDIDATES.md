# Recommended candidates (draft v2 — deep cuts)

Previous draft was too mainstream. This one stays in Cosmo D territory: small-dev, surreal, jazz/ambient-scored, liminal-space wandering games. None of these are in the current 46.

## Cosmo D himself

- **Off-Peak** — his first game (2015, free). Same universe as The Norwood Suite: a surreal train station bazaar, first pass at his wandering-collage style.
- **Tales from Off-Peak City Vol. 1** — same universe, first-person vignettes, deepens the world Norwood Suite is already logged for.
- **Betrayal at Club Low** — his latest (2023): dice-rolled dialogue, jazz-noir nightclub mystery, still the same fictional city.

## Jazz / cosmic surrealism

- **Genesis Noir** — Miles Davis-inspired cosmic jazz surrealism, the Big Bang told as a noir love story.
- **Superbrothers: Sword & Sworcery EP** — moody, minimalist, Jim Guthrie soundtrack; cult classic sound-design touchstone.

## Absurdist / comedic surrealism

- **Jazzpunk** — deadpan spy satire, dreamlike non-sequitur vignettes.
- **Sludge Life** — first-person skating/graffiti surreal open world, punk aesthetic.
- **Extreme Meatpunks Forever** — anarchic, mech-dating-sim-adjacent, punk narrative delivery.

## Liminal / fragmented horror-adjacent

- **Paratopic** — VHS-glitch liminal horror, deliberately fragmented narrative.
- **Mundaun** — hand-pencil-drawn folk horror, Swiss Alps setting.
- **The Longing** — solitary real-time meditative wandering (a 400-day single mechanic).

## Analgesic Productions (dream-logic auteur)

- **Anodyne** — surreal dream-logic Zelda-like.
- **Anodyne 2** — shifts between dream and reality states.
- **All Our Asias** — experimental structure, a memory-train narrative.
- **Even the Ocean** — political/emotional platformer narrative.

## Quiet / minimalist narrative

- **Wide Ocean Big Jacket** — quiet family camping trip, minimalist dialogue.
- **Wilmot's Warehouse** — abstract, meditative sorting-as-puzzle.
- **milk outside a bag of milk outside a bag of milk** — surreal, personal, very short.
- **Strange Horticulture** — small-scale atmospheric narrative puzzle.

## Metanarrative / genre pastiche

- **The Hex** — Daniel Mullins pastiching his own back catalog, metanarrative.
- **El Paso, Elsewhere** — Max Payne pastiche gone surreal noir.
- **We Know the Devil** — unsettling, tightly-scoped visual novel.

## Cryptic worldbuilding

- **Umurangi Generation** — photography mechanic, dense punk-cyberpunk lore delivered obliquely.
- **Dujanah** — surreal stop-motion claymation, grief/war allegory.

22 games, all small-team/indie, none owned in the Steam library snapshot (all fresh acquisitions). Say which to promote and I'll build the `games/<slug>/` stubs per CONTRIBUTING.md.
